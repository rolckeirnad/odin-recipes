Cheese and Burger Macaroni

Description

Homemade version of Hamburger Macaroni - just as easy and tastes so much better! You will need a large skillet with a tight fitting lid.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 small onion, minced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 8 ounces reduced-fat processed cheese food, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons reduced-fat sour cream

  1. Step 1

    Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef, onion, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper in the hot skillet until browned a crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.

  2. Step 2

    Return skillet to burner over high heat. Add water; cook skillet and bring to a boil. Stir macaroni into the boiling water-ground beef mixture; cover and lower heat to medium. Cook, keeping covered , for 5 minutes. Stir, cover, and cook until macaroni is tender, about 5 more minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Stir processed cheese food into ground beef-macaroni mixture until melted. Remove from heat; stir in sour cream.
